#+title: Behavioral Definition of Pretense is Difficult

One and the same behavior (eg. Child making "brrr" noises as it moves the toy car) can result from either Functional Play or Pretend Play.

This issue is an instance of [[id:06c35b0f-2cd8-4a16-bae3-2653dd8f5f7f][Non-identifiability of Behavioral Definitions]].
