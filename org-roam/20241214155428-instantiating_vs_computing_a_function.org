#+title: Instantiating vs Computing a Function

#+begin_quote
Planets going around the sun behave as /if/ they are following Newton's Laws. They are not themselves /representing or computing/ the Newton's Laws but only /instantiating/ them.

- [[http://krantisaran.net/][Kranti Saran]]
