#+title: Causal Learning through Culture in Children

Given the [[id:f9463952-6478-4ea8-91d6-4f856720a8d4][Cultural dependency of Causal Models]], the question remains: how do we - starting as children - acquire our understanding of the world from others?

The easiest answer is through language. Children don't just ask for labels of objects, but also ask /why/.
