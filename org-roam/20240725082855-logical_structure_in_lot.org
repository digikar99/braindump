#+title: Logical Structure in LOT

Some proponents of LOT claim that mentalese operates over the familiar logical connectives such as: and, or, not, if-then, there-exists, all. However, the exact logical language has changed from syllogistic, to propositional, to predicate (first order) logic, to probabilistic logic, and perhaps others. 

However, most (almost all?) mainstream formalisms suffer from [[id:063a9025-d908-4684-8631-f320cb2841d6][various problems]].
