#+title: Pretense is Dangerous

One expects that an organism's cognitive system would be [[id:d6eea95c-cc3c-44ef-8725-3fc69f7b2850][veridical]] with respect to the environment. Non-veridicality could mean that the organism mistakes poison for food and vice versa.
