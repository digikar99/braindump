#+title: Aristotle on Causation

Four irreducible causes:

1. Material cause: what is it made of?
2. Formal cause: what is it?
3. Efficient cause: where does change (or motion) come from?
4. Final cause: what is its good / why was it made?

Reference: https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/aristotle-causality/#FourCaus
