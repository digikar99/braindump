#+title: Non-Imperative Pointing

This is different from [[id:f76546ea-3368-408f-b2f4-a841c05f5ef8][Imperative Pointing]] and is primarily only seen in humans. This involves pointing for the sole purpose of directing the other's attention, or for informing them, or for requesting information.
